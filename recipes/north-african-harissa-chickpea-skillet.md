# North African Harissa & Chickpea Skillet

Harissa is a North African chili paste packed with roasted peppers, garlic, and warm spices like caraway and coriander. Combined with chickpeas, it creates a deeply flavorful, stew-like base for chicken.

## Equipment
- 1 large, deep skillet with a lid

## Yields
4 servings

## Ingredients
- 800g chicken breast, cut into large chunks
- 2 cans (400g each) chickpeas, rinsed and drained
- 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
- 3 tbsp harissa paste
- 150g fresh spinach
- Salt, pepper, and a squeeze of lemon juice

## Instructions
1. Heat the skillet over medium-high heat. Season the chicken chunks with salt and pepper, then sear them until browned (about 5 minutes).
2. Lower the heat to medium. Add the harissa paste and stir to coat the chicken.
3. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and the chickpeas. Stir well.
4. Cover and simmer for 15 minutes.
5. Remove from heat, stir in the fresh spinach until it wilts, and finish with a squeeze of fresh lemon juice.
