# Spicy Korean "Egg Roll in a Bowl"

This is a volume-eating cheat code. Cabbage cooks down significantly but retains a ton of bulk and fiber, making you feel incredibly full for very few calories. The gochujang (Korean chili paste) gives it a deep, savory heat.

## Equipment
- 1 large wok or deep skillet

## Yields
4-5 huge servings

## Ingredients
- 1 kg lean ground turkey or lean ground pork (5% fat)
- 1 massive head of cabbage, shredded (or 2 large bags of pre-shredded coleslaw mix)
- 3 tbsp soy sauce (or tamari)
- 2 tbsp gochujang (adjust for spice preference)
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- Fresh ginger and garlic (minced)
- Chopped green onions

## Instructions
1. Heat the wok over high heat. Add the meat, garlic, and ginger, cooking until the meat is browned.
2. Stir in the gochujang and soy sauce, mixing until the meat is coated.
3. Dump all the shredded cabbage into the wok. It will look like too much, but keep tossing it with the meat. It will wilt down in about 5 to 7 minutes.
4. Remove from heat, stir in the sesame oil, and top heavily with green onions.
